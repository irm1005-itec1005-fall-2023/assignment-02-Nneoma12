Hotels

A Hotel is a place that has rooms in which people can stay especially when they are traveling. It is a place that provides food, lodging, and other services for paying guests. The primary purpose of hotels is to provide travelers with shelter, food, refreshment, and similar services and goods, offering on a commercial basis things that are customarily furnished within households but unavailable to people on a journey away from home. There are various Hotels to consider on your stay, some are:

  • Residence Inn by Mariott
  • Four Points by Sheraton
  • Courtyard by Mariott

Restaurants

Restaurants are business establishments that provide foods, drinks, and snacks to customers. Restaurants vary greatly in appearance and offerings, including a wide variety of cuisines and service models ranging from inexpensive fast food restaurants and cafeterias, to mid-priced family restaurants, to high-priced luxury establishments. Customers are the most important part of a business, and they can be satisfied only if they get healthy and tasty food at nominal prices.There are various Restaurants to consider on your stay as good food could feed a craving soul, some good choices would be:

  • Harveys
  • East side Marios
  • Eggspectations
